About VOICE

A Smarter Way to Resolve Community Disputes

VOICE is Paete's digital barangay complaint management system — designed to make the formal complaint process faster, fairer, and more accessible for every resident. By digitizing the blotter system, we help barangay officials focus on resolution, not paperwork.

⚖️
Rooted in the KP Law
Fully aligned with the Katarungang Pambarangay Law under Republic Act 7160. Every workflow — from mediation to CFA issuance — follows proper legal procedure.
🤝
Community-First Design
Built for Paete residents of all digital literacy levels. Simple forms, clear status updates, and plain language notifications throughout.
📋
Full Transparency & Accountability
Every action on a case is logged. Officers, complainants, and respondents each see what they need — no more lost paper blotters or delayed notices.
📜
Katarungang Pambarangay (KP Law)
Under Sections 399–422 of the Local Government Code, all disputes between residents must first go through barangay-level mediation before being brought to court. VOICE digitizes and manages this entire mandatory process.

The Process

How VOICE Works

From filing to resolution — a clear, legally compliant process every step of the way.

📝
1
File a Complaint

A resident logs in and submits a blotter report with the incident type, narrative, location, and optional photo evidence.

🔍
2
Barangay Review

A barangay officer reviews the report, approves or routes it, and assigns the appropriate prescribed action.

🧑‍⚖️
3
Mediation Hearing

Both parties are notified of the hearing schedule. Attendance is tracked. Outcomes are recorded following KP Law guidelines.

✅
4
Resolution or Referral

Case is resolved, dismissed, or referred to the appropriate authority (PNP, VAWC, or Municipality) with a proper CFA if needed.
